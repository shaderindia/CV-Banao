Resume Writing Tips

📝 Writing Your Summary

Start with your years of experience, mention your specialization, and highlight 1-2 key achievements.

🎯 Technical Skills

List software proficiency levels, programming languages, and engineering tools you've used professionally.

💼 Work Experience

Use bullet points, start with action verbs, and include measurable results whenever possible.

🎓 Education

Include GPA if above 3.5, relevant coursework, and any academic honors or projects.
